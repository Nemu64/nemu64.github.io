Blog #18: The Time's Come

The topic for this week’s blog is the 9/11 attacks. The two main entities involved in the event were the US and Al-Qaeda. During the attacks, 4 passenger planes were hijacked and flown into various targets; two into the world trade centers, one into the Pentagon, and one which crashed into a field in Stonycreek Township in Pennsylvania though that wasn’t the intended target. The main location of the attacks was New York City where the most damage occurred, namely the two trade centers collapsing. This event is significant as it was it was the deadliest terrorist attack on in American history. It’s had long term effects such as the creation of the TSA and the Department of Homeland Security. The effects weren’t limited to the US however, with other countries implementing similar legislation, for example, the UK passed the Anti-terrorism, Crime and Security Act 2001. I decided to research this topic as I was hoping we would learn about it in HMUSH, but we simply ran out of time.
